Beautiful

October 14, 2023

Oh did you see that? It’s so beautiful!

And what about this?

Yes it’s so, well I can’t find the words for it but yes it’s so beautiful!

Wait a little longer.

What do you mean? It’s so beautiful!

We might discover something more if we hold our tongues.

Mmm, like this?

Yes, like this.
