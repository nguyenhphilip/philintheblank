Dwell

February 10, 2020

What rooms of the soul have we left unchecked Forgotten and locked away With the key nowhere to be found? Don’t worry. The door is still open. Climb the creaking stairs Open the dust-covered drawers and look in Run your fingers along the wooden shelves now draped in darkness, somewhere in the attic of your mind. Once there you may rediscover the very thing You have been searching for Your entire life. Prepare yourself: The ghosts will cry and beckon. But don’t run. Sit and stay awhile. Get to know yourself. Offer light and accept the forgiveness that soon follows.
